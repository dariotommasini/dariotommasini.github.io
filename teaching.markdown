## Graduate Teaching Assistant
**UC Berkeley** | *Department of Neuroscience*


### Neurobiology of Disease (Neuroscience 165)
*Spring 2025* | *Prof. William Jagust and Prof. Stephan Lammel*

An advanced course focusing on the molecular and cellular mechanisms underlying neurological and psychiatric disorders

* **Clinical Integration:** Facilitated discussions on primary literature on disorders such as Alzheimer’s, Parkinson’s, and Schizophrenia
* **Technical Assistance:** Managed the course's digital learning platform (bCourses) and coordinated grading of the exams via Gradescope

### Brain, Mind, and Behavior (Neuroscience C61)
*Spring 2024* | *Prof. David Presti and Prof. Robin Ball*

This foundational course explores the biological basis of behavior, covering topics from neuroanatomy to complex cognitive processes

* **Discussion Leader:** Led weekly sections for 20+ students, covering key concepts like action potentials and synaptic plasticity
* **Curriculum Support:** Designed supplemental study guides and interactive quizzes to reinforce lecture material
* **Mentorship:** Held office hours weekly to provide personalized academic support
